Trade Permission Logic

⚡ Day Trade Mode
  • TRADE ALLOWED — TRIGGERED or READY + Setup ≥6, Catalyst ≥4, Momentum ≥5, Vol ≥1.2x
  • WATCH — Setup forming, ≥4 score, not all conditions aligned yet
  • BLOCKED — Extended entry, Avoid bias, or scores too low
  • Prioritizes: intraday momentum, ORB breakouts, VWAP reclaims, volume spikes
📈 Swing Trade Mode
  • TRADE ALLOWED — A+ READY badge: Swing ≥7, actionable status, Catalyst ≥4, R:R ≥1.5
  • WATCH — Swing ≥4, setup still building
  • BLOCKED — REJECTED (weak structure) or EXTENDED (chasing)
  • Prioritizes: daily trend, 4H structure, EMAs, Fibonacci zones, catalyst quality
